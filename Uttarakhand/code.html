About Uttarakhand

Geography

Uttarakhand, located in the northern part of India, is known for its Himalayan ranges, glaciers, rivers, and dense forests.

History

Formerly part of Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand became the 27th state of India on November 9, 2000. It has a rich cultural heritage dating back centuries.

Demographics

Home to approximately 10 million residents with diverse ethnic backgrounds, languages, and traditions.
